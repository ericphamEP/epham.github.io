About Me
-------------------
Since taking computer science classes from high school, I have pursued my interests in this field for the past 5 years. In this time, I have learned from a variety of experiences that are listed in this site, including university courses as part of the computer science program at Carleton University, community events such as hackathons, CTF challenges ("Capture The Flag" team-based security competitions), and conferences. Currently, I am in 3rd year standing for computer science at Carleton University, having achieved an A+ in all COMP courses to date.

I also got some related experiences involving a programming-based summer camp where I helped elementary school students understand basic programming principles, lab research where I investigated the use of software for AI research, and a teaching assistant position for some introductory computer science courses. Recently, I was also given an amazing opportunity to work at Ciena as an Embedded Software Engineer where I got to experience working closely with a team in an agile environment and developing actual features to support testing for a real product.

These experiences have helped shape my interest in software development. I am specifically keen on web development, and cloud computing. However, I would love to explore more areas in the future, like machine learning and AI, 3D rendering, and data streaming.

I have had a lot of fun working on projects and getting to learn more from each one. I look forward to experiencing more in this field, learning from it, and contributing to it. I am always open to new opportunities.

I can be reached at epham321@gmail.com. Thanks for taking a look at my website!
